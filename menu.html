Special Dishes

01.

Malabar
Dum Biriyani

Malabar Dum Biryani is a fragrant rice dish from Kerala, slow-cooked with marinated meat, spices, and garnished with fried onions, raisins, and cashews. Its rich aroma and taste make it a festive favorite.

02.

Hyderabadi
Biriyani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a fragrant dish from Hyderabad, slow-cooked with marinated meat, spices, and garnished with fried onions and herbs. Its rich aroma and taste make it a royal favorite.

03.

65
Biriyani

65 Biryani is a spicy and flavorful dish that combines crispy Chicken 65 with fragrant biryani rice. Cooked with aromatic spices, it offers a unique blend of crunch and rich taste, making it a favorite fusion dish.

04.

Chicken
Pollicha Mandhi

Chicken Pollicha Mandi is a flavorful Arabian-inspired dish combining smoky grilled chicken with fragrant mandi rice. Slow-cooked with spices, it offers a rich aroma and a unique blend of textures, making it a delicious favorite.

05.

Beef
Biriyani

Beef Biryani is a rich and aromatic dish made with tender beef, basmati rice, and flavorful spices. Slow-cooked to perfection, it offers a deep, hearty taste, making it a favorite among biryani lovers.
